Oxygen Not Included Multiplayer Mod

This is an unofficial multiplayer mod for Oxygen Not Included, enabling co-op play through Steam and Epic Online Services (EOS).

Features

  • Host or join multiplayer colonies
  • Real-time synchronization of duplicants, structures, and interactions
  • Supports both Steam and Epic accounts
  • Cross-platform compatible (Windows, Linux, macOS)

Installation

Instructions will be available on the GitHub repository.

Privacy

This mod uses Epic Online Services (EOS) to authenticate players. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of EOS. See our Privacy Policy for more.
